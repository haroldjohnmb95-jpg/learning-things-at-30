Day 002 — June 29, 2026

Pausing. Not Quitting.

I tried to build a website today. Actually, I've been trying for a while now with the free tools I have. But I keep hitting walls. Code that doesn't work. Things that break. Things I don't understand.

I decided to pause. Not quit — pause.

I think I was aiming too high. Trying to build something big before I understood the basics. Trying to run before I could walk.

It's frustrating. I know what I want to build but the gap between what I see in my head and what I can actually make right now is... a lot.

But I'm not giving up. I know I'm not the only one who feels this way. And I know that if I keep going, even slowly, I'll get there.

So for now — I rest. I learn. I come back. Sooner or later.
